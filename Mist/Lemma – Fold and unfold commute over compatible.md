Let $\rho_1, \rho_2 \in \places$ be compatible places s.t. $\rho_1 \compat \rho_2$, and let $\F_1 \in \{ \fold\;\rho_1, \unfold\;\rho_1 \}$ and  $\F_2 \in \{ \fold\;\rho_2, \unfold\;\rho_2 \}$, then $\F_1$ and $\F_2$ commutes, and we say they are _compatible_, i.e.
$$
\F_1 \compat \F_2 \equiv \F_1 \circ \F_2 = \F_2 \circ \F_1.
$$
